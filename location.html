Northern Cascades

Nestled in western Washington, the Northern Cascades is home to some of the deepest powder in the country with an average snowfall of over 600" a year. Wild Sky Lodge operates on 50 acres of untouched wilderness with verts well over 2000ft. 140 miles away from Seattle will take you this close to what some would call a 'Winter Paradise'. We offer access to many different types of zones ranging from pillowy powder runs, cruisy tree runs, to steep, aggressive spine lines.
